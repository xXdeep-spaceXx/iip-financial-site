Our Story

Built on Honest Advice

Innovative Investment Professionals was founded in 1996 with one mission: help people solve their investment problems through an honest, straightforward approach.

Over nearly three decades, we've grown into a team of experienced professionals serving families across Albuquerque, Rio Rancho, Santa Fe, and throughout New Mexico. What hasn't changed is our core belief — that every client deserves a plan as unique as their life.

We don't sell products. We build relationships, ask the right questions, and design strategies around your goals — not ours.

MeetJerry Baca

Jerry Baca, a lifelong resident of Santa Fe, NM, has been in the financial services business for 24 years. He attained a bachelor's degree in finance in 1998. Jerry worked with the prestigious company of New York Life for 3 years before being employed with Allstate Financial.

Jerry's recent decision to become a partner with Innovative Investment Professionals is his best accomplishment yet. He is now able to offer his clients more choices of new innovative financial products with the world's top insurance carriers.

Jerry's emphasis with financial services is about establishing personal relationships with his clients. He enjoys meeting new people and spending time educating them on the importance of having a secure future and helping them with their options in choosing appropriate financial strategies for themselves and their families. His client base ranges throughout the beautiful state of New Mexico.
