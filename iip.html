About

Meet Christopher Rael

Founded 1996

Christopher Rael started Innovative Investment Professionals in 1996 with the intention of helping people "solve their investment problems" through an honest, straightforward approach.

Chris went into this industry entirely to help people, and takes their individual situations into account when doing so. He'll get you not only to retirement, but through it. He'll go the extra mile to help clients achieve their goals.

Christopher is honest, compassionate, generous, and a pretty funny guy. When not doing this work, he's a high school basketball coach. In his spare time, he enjoys traveling, reading, movies, and spending time outdoors.
