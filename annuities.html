What We Offer

Income You Can't Outlive

An annuity is a contract between you and an insurance company. You contribute funds — either in a lump sum or over time — and in return the company guarantees you a stream of income, either immediately or at a future date.

At IIP, we focus specifically on fixed and fixed index annuities — products that protect your principal while still allowing your money to grow. There is no stock market risk to your account value. You cannot lose what you put in.

For retirees or pre-retirees who want safety first and reasonable growth second, annuities are often the right tool for a portion of your portfolio.

🛡️

Principal Protection

Your account value is backed by the claims paying ability of the carrier — no market exposure to your core savings.

📅

Guaranteed Income Stream

Turn your savings into a predictable monthly or annual payment — for a set period or for life.

📈

Growth Potential

Fixed index annuities link growth to a market index, so you participate in upside without exposure to downside.

🧾

Tax-Deferred Growth

Earnings inside an annuity grow tax-deferred until withdrawal — helping your money compound faster.

Product Types

Which Annuity Is Right For You?

We work with a range of annuity types depending on your timeline, income needs, and risk tolerance.

Most Common

Fixed Index Annuity

Your account value is linked to the performance of a market index (like the S&P 500), but protected from losses. You earn credits when the index goes up — and never lose value when it goes down.

  • Zero floor on losses
  • Capped or participation-rate upside
  • Optional income riders for guaranteed lifetime income
  • Ideal for the 5–20 year pre-retirement window
Predictable Income

Fixed Annuity

A straightforward contract: you contribute funds, and the insurance company credits a fixed interest rate for a set term. Simple, transparent, and completely predictable.

  • Guaranteed fixed interest rate
  • No market exposure whatsoever
  • FDIC-equivalent protection via carrier reserves
  • Ideal for short-term safe money parking
Immediate Income

Single Premium Immediate Annuity (SPIA)

Convert a lump sum into a guaranteed income stream that starts immediately. Popular for retirees who need income now and want to eliminate the risk of outliving their savings.

  • Income begins within 30 days
  • Payments for life or a set period
  • Customizable payout options
  • Ideal for immediately replacing a paycheck
Future Income

Deferred Income Annuity (DIA)

Lock in a future income start date now. You contribute today and choose when payments begin — often 5, 10, or 15 years from now. The longer you defer, the larger the payment.

  • Highest income payout of any annuity type
  • Income date is locked in contractually
  • Works well as part of a phased income strategy
  • Ideal for ages 50–62 planning ahead

Common Questions

Annuity FAQs

Is my money locked in forever?
No. Most annuities have a surrender period (typically 5–10 years) during which withdrawals may incur a fee. However, most contracts allow 10% free withdrawals per year from day one, and all surrender charges disappear after the contract term ends.
What happens to my annuity when I die?
Your beneficiary receives the remaining account value or the death benefit specified in your contract — avoiding probate entirely. This passes directly to them, typically within 30 days of claim filing.
How is an annuity different from a CD?
Both protect principal, but annuities offer additional features: tax-deferred growth, guaranteed lifetime income options, death benefit protections, and — in the case of fixed index annuities — market-linked growth potential without market risk.
Are annuities taxed?
Growth inside an annuity is tax-deferred — you don't pay taxes on earnings until you withdraw them. When you do withdraw, gains are taxed as ordinary income. If you fund the annuity with after-tax dollars, your original contribution comes back to you tax-free.
What is an income rider?
An income rider is an optional add-on to a fixed index annuity that guarantees you a specific monthly income for life — regardless of how long you live or what the market does. It's essentially building a personal pension into your annuity contract.
